Guess the Word

To play this game, click on the 'Play!' button.
You will see an image. Try to guess what word was used to search for that image and enter your word in the field. Click Guess.

If you guessed wrong, try again or load more photos to give more clues as to what the word might have been. For every extra photo you load/extra guess, points will be deducted. Hover your mouse over the photo to zoom in :)
